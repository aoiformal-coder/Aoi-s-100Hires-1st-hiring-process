💡 Excited to share a few powerful takeaways from my recent conversation with my former colleague, Tamara Kempf from Cisco.

We love that AI is the talk of the town. 😎  
With our little agents and assistants doing the work for us (we wish!), our human skills are becoming irrepleacible.

Here are 3 key takeaways CS or tech leaders:

🤩 Human skills are still critical.  
No matter how advanced AI becomes, core human traits like emotional intelligence, adaptability, and business acumen are essential. As Tamara put it, “So much of leadership is EQ… your ability to meet your team where they are” makes all the difference.

🤩 Experiment & empower.  
Encourage your teams to experiment with AI. There’s no such thing as failing, only learning. Highlight and share wins and lessons (even when an idea flops!) for fun discoveries and innovation!

🤩 Resilience drives success.  
Career journeys are full of setbacks. Use every situation as a chance to grow, adapt, and find what you can control (even if it means starting a master’s degree during downtime, as Tamara did!)

🔥 Bonus:  
Give this episode a listen to hear an array of AI use cases that Tamara is currently working on with her CS team!

It may give you a few ideas to try out tomorrow!
