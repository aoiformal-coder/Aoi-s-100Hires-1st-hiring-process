The Hard Truth About Customer Success 💥💰

Most Customer Success teams are leaving money on the table. For their companies, for themselves, and even for their customers.

And it’s not because they don’t care.
It’s because they’re still playing defense, taking on the role of the corporate "goalie".

Too many CS team are stuck in relationship mode.

Putting out fires. Sending recaps. Checking boxes.

All while someone else defines the growth strategy.

Here’s the reality 👇
🚫 CS is not the department that keeps customers happy.
💰 CS is the department and drives the organizational mindset that drives revenue impact from existing customers.
🚀 Which of course means CS is an essential new component of the companies' growth engine.

But that only happens when leaders make bold moves.

💥 Here are the 5 bold moves I encourage every CS leader to consider (as I shared at the amazing #CS100 event)

1️⃣ Be the Voice of the Customer

🎙️ Don’t wait to be asked, be heard. Tell everyone the good and bad truths about how we are (or are not) helping our customers win.
You are the closest connection between your company and the people it serves.

2️⃣ Know Your Revenue Math

📊 Customer Success without financial fluency is just a component of OpEx.
Know your NRR, GRR, and expansion cold. 
Understand where you fit in the company's P&L statement, and how new account growth (and CAC) compares to existing client growth.

3️⃣ Push Product, Don’t Just Partner

⚙️ Partnership is good, but impact requires pressure.
Push Product with purpose. Bring insights about how product can impact TAM/SAM/SOM and measurable growth, versus feature requests that impact experience only.

4️⃣ Lead Sideways & Up

🤝 Leadership isn’t vertical, it's in every direction.
Build influence across peers and executives by knowing their KPIs, not just yours. 
Align your work to what drives the business forward and help others accomplish their KPIs too.
Great CS leaders don’t wait for alignment, they create it.

5️⃣ Share the Wins

🏆 Don’t keep success quiet.
Shine a light on your team, your customers, and your company.
Tell the stories that inspire belief and show impact.
Because when people see the wins, they believe in the mission.

At DISQO, we’re not waiting for permission.
We’re building CS as a revenue force, one that partners across Product, Sales, and Marketing to deliver measurable impact customers feel and the business can see.

And here’s the truth 💡
You don’t need permission to lead this way.
You just need courage.

🔥 Your Challenge This Week
💥 Which Bold Move will you double down on?
💥 Where can you stop playing defense and start driving growth?

Stop waiting for your seat at the table.
Build one. 💪
